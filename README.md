# soccer-cs-stats
simple comparisson and explorative data analysis of some soccer data.

### data source
Data from [Fuﬂballdaten.de](http://www.fussballdaten.de) and [Transfermarkt.de](http://www.transfermarkt.de). Postgres DB hosted on Heroku.

### content
Data contains currently 15 national teams* with its 391 national players and its market values. 68 championship or quali matches from the last 10 years (2006-2016).

* list of national teams: BELGIUM, CROATIA, CZECH_REPUBLIC, ENGLAND, FRANCE, GERMANY, IRELAND, ITALY, POLAND, RUSSIA, SLOVAKIA, SPAIN, SWEDEN, SWITZERLAND, UKRAINE
